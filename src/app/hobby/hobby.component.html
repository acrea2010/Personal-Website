ROCK CLIMBING

In December 2018, a good friend and I went on a rock climbing trip to Red Rocks and Joshua Tree National Park. This was probably as close to a dirtbag experience as we were gonna get, from camping out at Walmarts to eating micro-waved eggs and cheese and to clearing out the waste from the RV. The boulders were really challenging and I didn't manage to send the routes that I wanted to, but definitely will be back one day.
